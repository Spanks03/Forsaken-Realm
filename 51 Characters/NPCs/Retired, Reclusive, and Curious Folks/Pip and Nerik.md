_Physical Description:_ Nearly indistinguishable at first glance, these halfling twins both sport mop-like curls of dark brown hair, ruddy cheeks, and tunics that are always a little too loose from years of stretching ale bellies. The only clear difference is that Pip has a gap-toothed grin, while Nerik’s left ear has a noticeable notch from some half-remembered accident. They’re rarely sober, and almost never seen apart, arms slung over each other’s shoulders as they sway their way through town.

_Character:_ Pip and Nerik are Oar’s Break’s self-proclaimed _“bardic brothers,”_ though everyone else knows them as the drunks who can clear a tavern faster than a fire. They specialize in bawdy duets, belting out verses so off-key and poorly timed that it sounds like two cats fighting in a sack. Yet, in their own minds, they are master performers destined for fame. Tuck tolerates them because they pay their tabs (eventually), and Big Yolla enjoys egging them on, sometimes lifting one of them onto a table to get the room “properly entertained.”

Despite their antics, the twins have hearts of gold. They’ll happily share the last of their coin with a stranger, and they’ve got a knack for turning a drunken disaster into a night people remember fondly—if not fondly for their singing, then for the chaos that followed.
