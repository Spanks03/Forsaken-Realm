Retired Mercenaries

**Physical Description:**  
Brel is a barrel-chested quarter-orc with a grizzled beard and a noticeable limp, a souvenir from “the job with the wyvern.” His arms are still thick with muscle, though his knuckles are gnarled from decades of swordplay. Doma, a broad-shouldered human woman with short, iron-grey hair, wears her old leather armor more as a comfort piece than protection. A faded tattoo of a lion’s fang coils around her left bicep, half-lost under wrinkles and scars.

**Character Description:**  
The two bicker constantly, especially over card games, but it’s the kind of bickering that comes from years of shared blood, ale, and survival. Brel writes war stories in a heavy, calloused scrawl no one can read, while Doma edits them loudly with a glass of blackberry wine in hand. They’re sharp-eyed, fiercely loyal to the town, and protective of younger folks—offering unsolicited combat advice or stepping in when trouble brews. The porch of their cottage is littered with half-finished card games, wood carvings, and a rusted greatsword used to prop up flower baskets.
