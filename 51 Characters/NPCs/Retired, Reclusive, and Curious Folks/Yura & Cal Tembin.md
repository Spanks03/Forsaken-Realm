Halfling Siblings — Reclusive Pair Behind Shuttered Glass

![[Pasted image 20250802130405.png]]
**Physical Description:**  
Yura is the elder by minutes but carries herself like years. She wears long, layered shawls in muted earth tones, her short dark hair always tucked behind one ear. Her sharp eyes flit constantly between curtains and cracks, always watching. Call is leaner, paler, with wispy chestnut curls and a habit of wearing too many belts, each with pouches and tools no one’s seen him use. Both move with quiet, deliberate steps—like they’re afraid of being overheard by the floorboards.

**Character Description:**  
The Tembins rarely speak unless spoken to, and even then, they often reply with a question of their own. They claim no trade, yet somehow always have what they need. Some say they were once spies, others that they were cursed by a seer to "never sleep safely in the open again." They speak in hushed tones, finish each other’s sentences, and seem to know far more than they let on. Their windows remain shuttered day and night, though light occasionally glows behind the panes—usually blue. Despite their mystery, they’ve never caused trouble. Not openly.
