*Human — Retired Midwife*

![[OIP.webp]]
**Physical Description:**  
Grandmother Kesh is a wiry woman with sun-leathered skin and silver hair coiled into a long, practical braid. Her face is lined with the years, but her eyes remain alert — sharp as knitting needles and twice as likely to find your flaws. She dresses in layered shawls and long skirts patched with years of care, always smelling faintly of lavender, woodsmoke, and a hint of something medicinal. A carved birch cane rests against her knee more often than not, though few have seen her truly rely on it.

**Character Description:**  
Once the town’s midwife and still very much its opinion, Kesh no longer delivers babies but continues to deliver sharp truths and unsolicited advice with the same calm authority. She speaks in short, knowing phrases and has the uncanny habit of showing up exactly when you need her — or when you don’t. Though she rarely smiles, there’s warmth behind her words, even when they sting. Most folks say you haven't really settled into Oar’s Break until Kesh has told you what you're doing wrong.
