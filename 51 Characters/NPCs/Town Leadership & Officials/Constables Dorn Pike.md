*The Stern Lawman*

![[Pasted image 20250727145103.png]]
**Physical Description:**  
Dorn Pike stands stocky and unyielding, like the granite cliffs that border the nearby hills. His weathered face is deeply lined, each wrinkle telling tales of long years spent upholding law and order. His steel-gray beard is trimmed with precision, braided in the traditional dwarven style, though streaks of white speak to his advanced age. Sharp, piercing eyes miss nothing—scanning every room like a hawk searching for prey. His hands, thick and calloused, rest steadily on the worn pommel of his warhammer, a symbol as much as a tool.

**Character Description:**  
Dorn Pike is as stern as the stone he resembles. He speaks with a measured voice and expects discipline and respect from all who come before him. His judgment is fair but uncompromising, and his presence alone is often enough to quiet even the rowdiest of tavern crowds. Yet beneath his gruff exterior lies a kind heart—for those who come prepared and honest. He values readiness and responsibility, and those who show these traits find in Dorn a steady ally. His years as both constable and judge have earned him the wary respect of Oar’s Break’s residents, who know that while he rarely smiles, his sense of justice never wavers.
