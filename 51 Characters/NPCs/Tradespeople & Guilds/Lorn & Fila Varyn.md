**Physical Description:**  
Lorn Varyn is tall and lean half-elf, with dark hair often tied back and flecks of soot on his hands and face. His sharp, observant eyes reflect the steady focus of a skilled craftsman. 

Fila Mareth, shorter human with wild copper curls that catch the light like sparks, has nimble fingers that shape molten glass with practiced ease.

**Character Description:**  
Lorn and Fila work together like a well-oiled machine, blending quiet dedication with a shared passion for their craft. They’re modest and soft-spoken but carry a quiet warmth and determination. Their teamwork speaks volumes, and their bond shows in every delicate piece they create.
