**Physical Description:**  
Wilma is a small but commanding presence, with keen eyes that miss no detail. Her face is lined with years of salt air and hard work, framed by a mane of hair streaked with salt and pepper—though more salt now—tied back tightly with a frayed knot of old sail thread. Her weathered coat, faded and patched, is habitually thrown over the back of the same creaky chair by the dock office, a silent testament to long days spent managing the bustle of the waterfront.

**Character Description:**  
Wilma rules the docks with quiet, unwavering authority. Her sharp tongue and sharper mind make her a force to be reckoned with; she never repeats herself and speaks only when necessary. Every ship that slips into the harbor is cataloged in her memory—its name, captain, recent delays, and exact cargo weight down to the stone. Wilma’s control keeps the port running smoothly, preventing chaos from creeping into the daily comings and goings. She’s respected—sometimes feared—but always relied upon, and everyone knows that beneath her gruff exterior lies a steadfast guardian of the town’s lifeblood: the river and sea trade.
