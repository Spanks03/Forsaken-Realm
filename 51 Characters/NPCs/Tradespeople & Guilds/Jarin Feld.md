*Gnome — Reclusive Beekeeper*

![[il_1080xN.6007203816_71e9.webp]]
**Physical Description:**  
Jarin Feld is a small, stooped gnome with a weatherworn face and amber-flecked eyes that always seem to be watching something no one else can see. His curly auburn hair spills out wildly from beneath a broad weaved hat stained with wax and time. He wears a patchwork coat stitched with pockets—most holding tiny tools, dried herbs, or bits of honeycomb—and his boots are always dusted in pollen and mud. The low hum of bees often trails him, like a living cloak.

**Character Description:**  
Quiet and often half-lost in thought, Jarin speaks rarely and softly, as if not to disturb some greater rhythm only he perceives. Many in town claim he speaks to the bees—and that they answer. His apiary lies just beyond the edge of town, and though few visit, those who do report an eerie calm among the hives. Jarin is harmless, they say, but unnerving: he always seems to know when a storm is coming, or when someone’s lying. Whether this insight comes from instinct or insect whispers, no one’s quite sure.
