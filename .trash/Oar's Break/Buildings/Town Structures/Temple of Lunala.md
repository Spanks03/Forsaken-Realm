Nestled beneath a canopy of ancient whispering pines, the **Temple of Lunala** is a sanctuary of quiet shadows and soft silver light, where the night folds gently over the waking world. The temple’s walls are crafted from smooth, dark stone veined with mica that glimmers faintly like stars caught in rock. Silver candles—tall and slender—are arranged in delicate clusters, their flames flickering with a gentle, almost sentient glow that casts dancing patterns across the polished floors and veiled alcoves. The air is cool and carries the faint scent of night-blooming jasmine and worn parchment, as if the very breath of night has settled here.

At the heart of this serene refuge moves **[[Sister Nyra Vale]]** a calm and compassionate guide whose soft voice carries the weight of moonlit wisdom. Draped in flowing robes threaded with silver and violet, she tends to the faithful and the lost alike—offering moon-blessed charms woven with strands of soul fiber, whispered prayers to ease restless dreams, and gentle hands that steady those nearing final passages. To her, darkness is not to be feared but embraced; in its quiet majesty lies healing, reflection, and the sacred end of all things. The bell she rings echoes softly, a call to stillness and reverence.

The temple is a living prayer to **[[Lunala]], the Keeper of Night and Darkness**—the great Lunar Dragon whose argent-black wings veil the world in rest and mystery. Here, beneath the crescent sigil that cradles five stars representing dreams, death, secrets, night, and magic, worshipers come seeking solace, guidance, and the slow revelation of hidden truths. Soft chants rise in the midnight hour as moonlight pools in silver basins, mirroring the stars above and reminding all that night is not absence, but the cradle of wisdom and renewal.

In the shadowed corners of the temple, the Umbral Veil oracles whisper of dreams yet to unfold, and the faithful prepare for the Moonveil Procession—a silent, lantern-lit walk through the town that honors those who rest and the gentle powers guarding the dark hours. This is a place where endings are sacred, secrets are kept safe, and the patient heart learns to see by the light of silence.
