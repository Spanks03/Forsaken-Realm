## The God of Revenge: Vimnera

_"Revenge is a powerful motivator."_  
  

Revenge is a dark motivator and is hard to misunderstand. There are many ways to fall into Vimnera's hands, but there is always the same goal: vengeance and retribution. Many people view Vimnera as a poison that corrupts loved ones, but the people who need her are grateful for the freedom and the focus to help correct wrongdoings.

### Vimnera's History

The birth of Vimnera is a response to an act of conflict. Ogul's creation of Temp rolled fury through Terra and Nosduh retaliation was the goddess of revenge. As Nosduh's third creation, Vimnera goal was clear. The goddess not took revenge on her sibling with her twisted plots but also took revenge on the Elder Gods for their creation and are rumored to have started the cataclysm.

Vimnera created the yuan-ti, lizardfolk, goblins, hobgoblins, and bugbears to set out cleaver and devious warfare throughout Terra. She imprinted the importance of a long term plan without the remorse of collateral destruction on these creatures and plagued the land of revengeful actions.

During the cataclysm, Vimnera twisted her sibling against their creators. Some may have seen her plans before others and some were lucky to be on the better side, but she still had the power to make all take action. The Goddess sunk her teeth into Quathos and Temp, instigating the first blow against the Elder Gods. For her reprehensible actions, she was the third god imprisioned as khomos, the Apex Predator as her punishment. Her contribution to Khomos is as dangerous as if she was still free.

### Vimnera's Influence

Only one large group follows Vimnera, the Penumbra Syndicate, but most people have personal vengeance that they need to do alone. There are clerics of Vimnera called **Retribution's Call** that find people that need help with their revenge. The clerics travel by themselves and are found where people are at their lowest and need help starting and planning their revenge. Led by Archbishop Ravthek, a famous assassin shrouded in mystery and known for their ability to change their form at will and get into anywhere unseen and unheard.

Vimnera does not mind the quick tit-for-tat but truly supports a long, planned-out retribution that causes a ripple in the target's history.

### Vimnera's Temples

The locations of worship for Vimnera are hard to notice to the normal person. Most from the outside look like a delicate stone arch way in a field with ivy rapped up its pillars. When walking near the arch with the need for revenge in your heart, a doorway to a stairway down to the temple will open. The temples are rumored to be enchanted to protect them from being destroyed. Some sprout miles of hedge mazes around the people coming near with bad intention, other create false memories of friends or loved ones betrayal for them to seek revenge. Vimnera's temples are on the smaller size but could be helpful in seeking revenge. The main chamber will have a stone depiction of Vimnera with beautiful sculptures of snakes climbing her legs. The main chamber will normally be full of poisonous plants that could be used to kill or do all sorts of harm to a being. The side chambers for locking in victims or torturing. Some examples of famous Vimnera Temples are the Omen Marsh, the Luminescent Crypts, and the Snow's Arch.

The Omen Marsh is located almost a two days ride west from the rail of Merrimarsh along the coast. Originally named from when a traveling bard named Viollette Mevit found goblin metal in the marsh and told the tale to Merrimarsh as a curse omen. What first started as a way for a bard to make money became a precurser to the Assault of Kninad. In these same marsh, it is known that all that is left of the area is mashed down earth and bones from the assault and a Vimnera Arch. The marsh will grapple the feet of the being that try to destroy the arch.

The Luminescent Crypts are located on the outskirts of Sousey. Also known as a burial sight for the Legion. This is a location that the archway was destroyed by a group hired by the Legion called the Division. The only reason that this temple has not been lost in time is because it is found frequently by the soldiers that are walking through lighting their loved ones torches in front of their graves and they have revenge in their hearts.

The Snow's Arch is located north of Netherfield in the Vontrie Snow Plains. It known as one of the most beautiful walks of in all of Terra. The lights from beyond the Poindrentym Ridge create the crystal of the snow to sparkle. Many couples make this walk to get engaged, married, or a romantic outing. It is also used for lover to take revenge on their partner for a wrong doing. If walking toward the arch with malintent, a memory of a horrific betrayal from a loved one will be seared in that being's mind and revenge will take their heart.
### Vimnera's Artifact

When Vimnera teared through Terra, she create a set of light chain mail called _The Links of Vengefulness_. Vimnera set her eyes on a champion early on named Iernas Suthrum. Iernas had a vengeance burning in his soul but had nowhere to point it because he never knew who wronged him. Vimnera fell in love with his fury and created a set of light chain mail out of her veil.

After time has rushed by and words became myth, the Links of Vengefulness sat for a period in Iernas Suthrum tomb that is a days ride north west from Deep Oak. Some say it is still draped on Iernas's bones and other say it was critically used by Gerald Halfstrider in the last battle of Deep Oak. There are even painting depiction of him wearing some interesting chain mail under his plate in a group painting of all that was in the exploration into the Void. Pray to the Gods if it made its way back to Vimnera.

The item card for this artifact will be in appendix A.

### Vimnera's Children

Just like her siblings, Vimnera also had a handfull of children throughout the millenium of her time on Terra. Vimnera's love for Iernas Suthrum resulted in a child before his death. Nyrella, their little princess, became the Princess of Death and her first soul that she shepherd was her father.

Nyrella looks like a tall skinny women wearing dark layered robes that cover most of her skin. A porcelain white full face mask with delicate engraving that only stand out due to the dried blood recessed in the grooves. A thin veil draping off a bone crown that looks torn at the knees but when touched feels as strong as chainmail. Occasionally you will see a glimmer of her eyes in the dark eye pockets of the mask.

#### Vimnera's Champions

**Alignment:** Mostly lawful, often evil  
**Suggested Classes:** Barbarian, Blood Hunter, Cleric, Forsaken, Paladin, Rogue  
**Suggested Cleric Domains:** Death, Tempest, Trickery, War **Suggested Background:** Criminal, Hermit, Haunted One, Noble, Urchin  
Most champions of Vimnera as surprisingly cheerful and kind, hiding the darkness they hide. These avengers are virtually indistinguishable from the ordinary person; however, they could kill a person instantly, so long they meet their prerequisites. They have a strict code of honor that they stick to, each tailored to their own personal values, but they all center around performing acts of revenge.

#### Earning and Losing Piety

You increase your piety to Vimnera when you honor her or give retribution for a wrongdoing through an act such as these:

- Killing a murderer
- Stealing from a scammer
- Destroy the life and career of a corrupt politician

---

Your piety score to Vimnera decrease if you fall into forgiveness or postpone vengeance through acts such as these:

- Allowing a criminal to escape
- Unjustly dealing your own justice
- Switching sides when you have a contract

#### Vimnera's Devotee

_Piety 3+ Vimnera trait_  
  
You've learned that one of the most basic lessons in revenge is proper timing. You can cast _hellish rebuke_ with this trait, requiring no material components, a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once). You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for this spell.
#### Vimnera's Votary

_Piety 10+ Vimnera trait_  
  
As your experience in retribution grows, you've come to realize a swift justice is just as effective as a planned one. When a creature within 5 feet of you makes an attack against a target other than you, you can use your reaction to make a weapon or melee spell attack against the attacking creature.

#### Vimnera's Disciple

_Piety 25+ Vimnera trait_  
  
Your devotion to vengeance has created a sixth sense within you where you can sense when reprisal is required for your allies. When you or an ally you can see within 30ft of you is hit with an attack, you can make the attacker roll a Constitution saving throw (DC equals 8 + your primary ability modifier + your proficiency bonus). On a failure, the creature receives as much damage as it dealt, except the damage is force damage. On a success, they take half the damage. This damage can't be reduced in any way.  
You can use this ability twice per long rest.

#### Champion of Retribution

_Piety 50+ Vimnera trait_  
  
You've devoted yourself to being a holy avenger, someone who has become exceptional at the art of doling out backlash. When you see a creature deal enough damage to make either you or an ally go below half their hit point maximum, you can immediately take an extra turn, interrupting the current turn. On this turn, you can move as far as you need to make 3 melee attacks or cast 2 spells. Any damage dealt this way can't be reduced in any way and bypasses all immunities and resistances.  
You can use this ability once every 1d6 days.
