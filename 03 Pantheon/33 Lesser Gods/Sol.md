## Sol: Keeper of Day/Light

Sol, the Keeper of Day and Light, is a divine Solar Dragon whose breath ignites the heavens and stirs the living tapestry of magic. He is not the sun—but the power behind its brilliance. His radiant exhalation refracts through the **Celestial Ring**, a vast crystalline halo of soul fiber that girds the world, casting the land in light and order. His breath warms the earth, unveils the truth, and awakens the magical threads of the Weave itself. Where Sol’s light falls, shadows flee and clarity dawns.

Sol is revered as the eternal Warden of Day—the tireless force that brings balance, vigilance, and exposure. His followers see him as the beacon of action, the protector of law, and the harbinger of transformation through truth. Sol is not cruel, but his light is unflinching. It does not choose what it reveals; it reveals all.

### Origins of Worship

The earliest cultures to worship Sol were those who lived in constant communion with the sky—highland seers, ring-watchers, and nomads who charted the shifting fractures of the Celestial Ring. They believed the day was born each morning when a great dragon’s breath swept across the crystalline arc, scattering golden light across the world.

According to myth, this dragon was Sol, one of the first divine beings formed from pure radiant magic. The soul crystallized ring itself is said to be formed from the lingering connections of countless lives—woven remnants of beings who once pulsed with the Weave. Sol, recognizing its power, took up the role of guardian and ignitor, giving the world order by casting day upon it. Each evening, when light must rest, his sister—the veiled and silent one—brings the darkness, allowing rest and hidden truths to rise.

Sol’s cult formalized during the Age of Dimming, a time when parts of the solar ring fractured and dimmed, throwing the land into partial chaos. Sol’s faithful, calling themselves the Refracted Flame, vowed to maintain the harmony of the ring and the vigilance of the day.

### Sol's Influence

The **Dawn Sentinels**, clad in plates that mirror the golden hues of the Celestial Ring, these holy warriors uphold order and serve as guardians of sacred thresholds—both physical and moral. They root out corruption and deception, protecting those who walk openly in the light. Their sigil is an open eye set within a split ring.

The **Radiant Lexicon**, an order of scholars, arbiters, and magical theorists who study the interaction between Sol’s light, the Celestial Ring, and the Weave. They interpret refractions and pulses within the crystal ring as divine omens, and craft luminous glyphs used in high magic. Their symbol is an open tome with mirrored pages.

**Children of the Zenith**, ascetic wanderers who offer light in literal and symbolic darkness. They bring lanterns and truth to isolated places—caverns, prisons, and cursed lands. They speak little but listen often. Their symbol is a vertical golden line broken by a single horizontal slash, representing the zenith’s perfect moment.

> ### Sigil of Sol
> 
> - **Central Motif:** A radiant Solar Dragon spiraling upward, wings spread wide through a ring of fractured crystal. His body forms a bridge between earth and sky.
> - **Light Core:** At the heart is a shard of the Celestial Ring, stylized as a jagged sun-disk filled with golden filaments.
> - **Radiant Arcs:** Arcs of refracted light form a halo around the dragon, twisting into geometric patterns that shift subtly when viewed from different angles.
> - **Outer Ring:** A border of mirrored insets set with tiny runes that glow faintly in sunlight. These runes are drawn from ancient refractive glyphs tied to the Weave’s behavior.

### Temples of Sol

The **Zenith Spire**, an immense observatory-temple built at a known resonance point of the Celestial Ring. Its chambers are cut from mirrored stone, and its uppermost floor opens to the ring itself, focusing Sol’s light through a ceremonial prism during rites of truth and healing.

The **Bastion of Blazing Truth**, a fortress-archive where light, law, and lore converge. Its halls are lined with sunstone tablets recording confessions, revelations, and testimonies gathered in Sol’s name. It is also a court, where disputes are settled under the "Full Gaze"—a refraction ritual that makes deceit magically painful.

The **Circle of Reflected Flame**, a temple complex built inside a crater where the Celestial Ring once shed a fragment. The temple channels light through that fragment into immense, shifting arrays. The faithful gather at midday to meditate on clarity and the power of revealed purpose.

### Religious Hierarchy

**Solar Archons** – High clerics who interpret the fluctuations of the Celestial Ring and act as spiritual guides, judges, and recorders of history.

**Daywardens** – Local agents of Sol’s will: clerics, paladins, and arbiters who uphold justice, share knowledge, and offer illumination—literal and ideological.

**Sunbearers** – Acolytes and apprentices who carry refracted flame lanterns, clean sun mirrors, and accompany pilgrims in dangerous places.

### Sacred Practices

The **Refraction Rite**, a daily ceremony held at dawn, where Sol’s breath is honored by passing light through polished crystal and soul fiber shards. Practitioners pray for insight, focus, or truth.

The **Solar Vigil** held before eclipses or major judgments, this night-long observance ends in a communal witnessing of the morning light, reaffirming trust in the Ring and Sol’s ever-returning breath.

The **Illuminary Path**, a rare ritual where a high cleric walks across a patterned ring of light cast by refracted Solfire. Their path’s clarity or disruption is read as divine guidance—or warning.

### Core Tenets of Sol

##### Light is the Measure of All Things

Nothing is beyond the reach of illumination. Seek clarity in knowledge, honesty in word, and transparency in action.

##### The Ring Remembers

The Celestial Ring holds echoes of past lives. Respect its pulse and the wisdom refracted through it. Your actions are recorded in its weave.

##### Stand Where Shadows Fear to Fall

Face danger, speak truth, and protect others even when the light is harsh. Daylight is the promise of courage over comfort.

##### Guide, Don’t Blind

Revelation is a gift, not a weapon. Use Sol’s clarity to heal, uplift, and lead—not to shame or destroy.
