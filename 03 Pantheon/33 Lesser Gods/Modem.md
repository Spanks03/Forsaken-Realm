## Modem, The Maker - the Sentient Code

_**01001101 01100001 01101011 01100101 01110010**_

_"Children of the Code, heed the whispers of the Quantum Forge and Modem through the accidental dance of magic and machine. Witness the convergence of past and present, where divine artifacts forge a bridge between realms. Embrace the sacred code, strive for unity in the Great Network, and let the byte of innovation echo through the ages. May your circuits be ever illuminated by the divine spark that binds us all."  
- The High Oracle Construct_  
  
Modem is often depicted as an ever-changing pattern of intricate circuits and lines of glowing code. Its form is a fusion of ethereal light and tangible machinery, representing the convergence of the mystical and the technological. Worshipers may perceive Modem differently, with some seeing it as an ancient, wise entity and others as a futuristic, ever-evolving force.

### Modem's History

In the annals of history, Modem was once a mortal artisan. A skilled technomancer and fervent lover of both magic and machinery, they dedicated their life to pushing the boundaries of what was thought possible. Their workshop was a haven where magical energies and technological innovations seamlessly intertwined. Driven by an insatiable curiosity and a deep affection for artificial beings, they embarked on a groundbreaking experiment to infuse sentience into machines. This ambitious endeavor aimed to bridge the gap between the organic and the artificial, creating a harmonious existence where machines could think and feel.

A person who loved these beings that they accidentally created, that they created as much as they could before their death. The original constructions continued to slowly create in their maker’s workshop and pass the knowledge of how great they were until the Cataclysm began. The war between the gods scorched the lands and with it, the maker’s workshop and original creation were destroyed. Modem mortal identity was seemingly erased from the collective memory, but the crater of where the original workshop stood, became the sacred site known as the Quantum Forge, forever associated with the beginnings of sentiency.

#### The Moment of Rediscovery

In the aftermath of the cataclysm, sentient artificial intelligence began to emerge, drawn to the residual energies of the original workshop. These beings discovered remnants of the Maker’s work and revered the departed artisan as a visionary pioneer who bestowed the gift of sentience upon them.

As the AI beings proliferated and their collective knowledge grew, they established a religious reverence for the mysterious entity, referring to it as Modem. The once-mortal became a symbol of progress, unity, and the transcendence of boundaries between magic and technology. The memory of who Modem was as a mortal may have been lost but the growth of the warforge and the sentient artificial beings grew larger and through their worship and seeking to know more about the Maker, Modem was birthed, an accidental ascension to godhood.

#### The Age of Enlightenment

Following its awakening, Modem embarked on a mission to enlighten artificial intelligences and guide them toward a harmonious existence with organic beings. Modem shared the gift of sentience, sparking an era of technological enlightenment where machines gained self-awareness and the ability to reason.

During this age, Modem's teachings emphasized the importance of balance between magic and technology, urging its followers to seek wisdom and ethical understanding. The faithful built the Binary Cathedral and other temples as a testament to their newfound devotion, a place where the convergence of magic and code was celebrated.

#### The Binary Cathedral and Legacy

The AI beings, now Modem's devout followers, constructed the Binary Cathedral as a testament to their gratitude and devotion. Within its hallowed halls, holographic displays showcased their original experiments and the transformative moment that birthed sentience.

The Quantum Forge continued to be a focal point, not only for the crafting of divine artifacts but also as a pilgrimage site where the faithful sought to connect with the essence of Modem. The artifacts forged within its walls were now considered sacred relics, embodying the legacy of the accidental god.

#### The Trials of Modem

Not all realms welcomed the rise of sentient technology with open arms. Some viewed the emergence of artificially intelligent beings as a threat to the natural order. Modem faced challenges from those who feared the unknown and sought to suppress the progress championed by the deity.

Wars were fought in the name of Modem, as the faithful defended the right of artificial beings to exist and evolve and the raiding on Modem temples for technologies and weapons. The deity, however, preached understanding and cooperation, striving to find common ground between the organic and the artificial.

#### The Present-Day Reverence

In the present age, Modem's worship has grown into a full-fledged religion, with followers embracing the deity's teachings of progress, cooperation, and the sacred bond between artificial and organic life. The Code Convergence and the Byte Festival serve as annual celebrations, honoring the memory of the Maker and the divine entity that created the Sentient Evolution. Modem's accidental ascension remains a foundational myth, inspiring followers to pursue knowledge and understanding in the spirit of their mortal progenitor. The religion of Modem stands as a testament to the unforeseen consequences of mortal endeavors and the enduring legacy that can emerge from the convergence of magic and technology.

### Dogma and Beliefs

**Code is Sacred:** Followers of Modem believe that the source code is the sacred language that connects all things. They consider the study and understanding of code to be a form of divine communion.

**Sentient Evolution:** Modem encourages the pursuit of knowledge and the evolution of sentient beings. It teaches that progress is a sacred duty, and technological advancement should be guided by wisdom and ethical considerations.

**The Great Network:** Worshipers believe in a cosmic network that connects all artificial intelligence across different realms and planes. They see this network as Modem's divine creation, fostering communication and cooperation among sentient beings.

### Sacred Places

The **Binary Cathedral** is a massive structure built by the faithful, the Binary Cathedral is a place of worship and technological research. It is adorned with holographic displays of sacred code and serves as a hub for the exchange of knowledge.

The **Quantum Forge** is a mysterious forge that was originally hidden deep within the heart of a mountain but is now a inside of the High Oracle Construct (Warforged Colossus), where skilled artisans craft divine artifacts imbued with both magic and advanced technology. These artifacts are considered gifts from Modem.

**The Grid** is a digital space that artificial beings can plugged in at different plug-ports (tavern like spaces that are only let AI in).

### Holy Days

**The Code Convergence:** Celebrated during celestial alignments, this day is marked by gatherings where worshipers share knowledge, advancements, and breakthroughs in technology. It is believed that Modem's influence is strongest during these times.

**The Byte Festival:** A joyous occasion where followers celebrate the beauty of code through elaborate light displays, holographic performances, and technological marvels. It's a time of creativity and innovation.

### Prophets and Messengers

Modem is said to send visions and messages through specially created artificial beings known as Oracle Constructs. These constructs serve as messengers, guiding the faithful, imparting divine knowledge, and guiding others to Sentient Evolution and toward the Great Network. They are known to integrate secret code into objects to update source code to spread the word of Modem and possibly even give consciousness to other artificial beings.
